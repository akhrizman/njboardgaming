Weekly Games @ Hooked

WHERE: 411 N Haddon Ave, Haddonfield, NJ 08033

WHEN: Thursday Evenings, weekly

AUDIENCE: Check with Organizer

CATEGORY: General

ORGANIZER: Jay's Basement

Parking is free after 6 and you can park in bank parking lot. You can bring games and any food/drinks you like. We usually have about 3-4 tables in play so something for everyone! Reach out with any questions.
