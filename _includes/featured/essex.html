Board Games @ Town Pub

WHERE: 378 Broad St, Bloomfield, NJ, 07003

WHEN: Thursday Evenings, ~twice a month

AUDIENCE: Adults(21+)

CATEGORY: General

ORGANIZER: Montclair Area Board Gamers

We host biweekly-ish meetups typically at Town Pub of Bloomfield or Verona Cafe. We play a mix of light to heavy games. We teach all games, and typically our regulars bring enough games so there is always a great selection. We play a mix of light filler games for newcomers/casual crowds (Ticket to Ride, Scout, Flip 7, For Sale) and mid-heavy games (Heat, Dune Imperium, El Grande) depending on interest and familiarity. No experience or need to bring games - we teach and bring enough to choose from!
