Board Games @ Town Pub

WHERE: 378 Broad St, Bloomfield, NJ, 07003

WHEN: Thursday Evenings, ~twice a month

AUDIENCE: Adults(21+)

CATEGORY: General

ORGANIZER: Montclair Area Board Gamers

We host biweekly-ish meetups typically at Town Pub of Bloomfield or Verona Cafe. We play a mix of light to heavy games. We teach all games, and typically our regulars bring enough games so there is always a great selection. We play a mix of light filler games for newcomers/casual crowds (Ticket to Ride, Scout, Flip 7, For Sale) and mid-heavy games (Heat, Dune Imperium, El Grande) depending on interest and familiarity. No experience or need to bring games - we teach and bring enough to choose from!

Board Games @ Time Warp Comics and Games

WHERE: 555A Pompton Ave, Cedar Grove, NJ 07009

WHEN: 1st & 3rd Sundays 12:00PM-10:00PM

AUDIENCE: Adults(18+)

CATEGORY: General

ORGANIZER: Jersey Association of Gamers

Jersey Association of Gamers (JAG) is a group of adults who get together twice a month to play board games including “euros”, war games, and “ameritrash”. Our meetings are held at Time Warp Comics in Cedar Grove, NJ. Game play usually starts soon after noon with a second round of gaming starting in the midafternoon. During the fall of every year we ask members who attend meetings regularly to contribute ($20) to cover the cost of JAG’s Meetup group subscription. Thank you to the many members who have already contributed.
