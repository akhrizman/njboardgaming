Board Games on the Casual @ Panera

WHERE: 510 Nassau Park Blvd, Princeton, NJ 08540

WHEN: Wednesdays 7:00PM-10:00PM

AUDIENCE: Check with Organizer

CATEGORY: General

ORGANIZER: Princeton Board Games on the Casual

The drinks are good, the tables are big and empty, and the people are friendly. You're welcome to bring your own game or join one on someone else's, we always have plenty. If you're a veteran gamer, you'll find plenty of company. If you're brand new or just want to expand your horizons, you'll find that everyone is excited to include and teach new players. If you're not sure where to start, look for me, Scott, or Jess, and we can help you find a game we think you'll enjoy. We usually have about 25-35 people each week, so there's always a game looking for a new player! PLEASE NOTE: We do not meet when Panera is Closed (i.e. Christmas, New Years, etc.) Always check our Meetup Page for the official event.
