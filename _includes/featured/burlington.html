Board Games @ The Guild Hall

WHERE: 400 NJ-38 #1360, Moorestown, NJ 08057

WHEN: Sundays 12:00PM-6:00PM

AUDIENCE: Check with Organizer

CATEGORY: General

ORGANIZER: Voorhees Boardgames Meetup Group

Come play Board Games at The Guild Hall Hobby in the Moorestown Mall. All games in their extensive library of over 600 games are FREE to play on Sundays. You are also welcome to bring your own games to play. The Guild Hall Hobby website has a list of most of the games in their library, but sometimes new release games may be available to play that haven't been added to the list yet. If you haven't been here in a while note that they have moved from their previous location and are now directly across from the food court, where FYE used to be. If you enter the mall via the food court just keep walking straight into the main section of the mall and they are straight ahead, just down from where they they used to be. It is very helpful if you RSVP if you plan to come as this event may be canceled if no one other than the organizer signs up for it.
