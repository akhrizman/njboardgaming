Board Games @ Seaview Baptist Church

WHERE: 2025 Shore Rd, Linwood, NJ 08221

WHEN: 1st FRIDAY, MONTHLY 6:00PM-10:30PM

AUDIENCE: Check with Organizer

CATEGORY: General

ORGANIZER: South Jersey Board Gaming League

Come join us for the monthly SJBGL Game Night! We have games or bring your own - all are welcome! We normally play games until 10:30-ish, so that we can clean up and lock up by 11. There are usually between 10 - 20 gamers in attendance with 3 or 4 games being played simultaneously at different tables. A few of the regulars normally being a couple of snacks, but there are many restaurants nearby if you’re needing real food.

Board Game Night @ Garden State Brewery

WHERE: 247 E White Horse Pike, Galloway, NJ 08205

WHEN: Every other TUESDAY 5:00PM-9:00PM

AUDIENCE: Adults(18+)

CATEGORY: General

ORGANIZER: South Jersey Board Game Time (Discord)

We are just a group of people who play all kinds of board games. We always like to try new board games, but we have regular games we play like Wingspan, Quacks, Everdell, Flip 7, Splendor and more! If there are a bunch of us we break up into groups, if there aren't as many we try to find games that works for everyone! Bring your own board games, or enjoy the board games some of the other board gamers bring. If you don't use Discord, we usually post updates about our event on South Jersey Board Gaming League's Facebook page. Hope to see you there!
