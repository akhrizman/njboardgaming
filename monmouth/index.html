Featured

The following is a list of events or meet ups that occur regularly. For more information, please contact the organization directly. Featured events must always remain free and be open to the general public. We are not involved in the organization of events or how they are run, but our mission is to promote a welcoming environment, especially for people just getting into the hobby. If you have any concerns about an event or organization or find that they are no longer active, please don't hesitate to contact us

BoardgamesNJ @ Holy Innocents Spiritual Center

WHERE: 3455 W Bangs Ave, Neptune City, NJ 07753

WHEN: ~One Friday a month 5:00PM - 10:00PM

WHO: Family Friendly

CATEGORY: General

ORGANIZER: Boardgames NJ Meetup Group

Todd has arranged for space in the Holy Innocents Spiritual center for a gathering of gamers, and has kindly welcomed BoardgamesNJ to join in the fun. End time is flexible, and feel free to arrive anytime after 5 pm. If you'd like to bring some yummies to share, please do! Pizza will be available. A donation box will be available to help buy pizza for the next one! Come one, come all!

Beers & Board Games @ Source Brewing

WHERE: 300 RT-34, Colts Neck, NJ 07722

WHEN: Once a month on Friday Night or Saturday afternoon

WHO: Adults (21+)

CATEGORY: General

ORGANIZER: Monmouth Boardgame Enthusiasts

Meet at Source Brewing in Colts Neck to share some beers and board games, 21+ only. It would be great to see you there! We usually play more casual games that are under 90 minutes and easy to learn. Great way to break into board gaming for newer folks. Enjoy a board game with the brewery drinks. No outside beverages (including water bottles), they have water and seltzers for non-alcoholic options. BYO Food: bring lunch or snacks.
