Featured

The following is a list of events or meet ups that occur regularly. For more information, please contact the organization directly. Featured events must always remain free and be open to the general public. We are not involved in the organization of events or how they are run, but our mission is to promote a welcoming environment, especially for people just getting into the hobby. If you have any concerns about an event or organization or find that they are no longer active, please don't hesitate to contact us



Board Games @ Town Pub

WHERE: 378 Broad St, Bloomfield, NJ, 07003

WHEN:Thursday Evenings, ~twice a month

FOR: Adults(21+)

CATEGORY: General

ORGANIZER: We host biweekly-ish meetups typically at Town Pub of Bloomfield or Verona Cafe. We play a mix of light to heavy games. We teach all games, and typically our regulars bring enough games so there is always a great selection. We play a mix of light filler games for newcomers/casual crowds (Ticket to Ride, Scout, Flip 7, For Sale) and mid-heavy games (Heat, Dune Imperium, El Grande) depending on interest and familiarity. No experience or need to bring games - we teach and bring enough to choose from!
